# KulimaVerse: Behavioral Psychology & Trust Design

The KulimaVerse Behavioral Psychology layer is designed to align individual self-interest with the collective goal of honest, high-quality impact verification. We use a combination of economic incentives, social proof, and subtle nudges to create a "Trust-First" culture.

---

## 1. Incentives & Discouragement

### Incentives for Good Behavior (The "Carrot")
*   **Priority Funding**: High-trust nodes (TS > 0.9) get their funding tranches released 48 hours faster.
*   **Lower Collateral**: As trust increases, the required VU stake for participation decreases.
*   **Governance Weight**: Trust Score is a multiplier for voting power in the Federated DAO.
*   **Visibility Alpha**: High-trust projects are featured on the "Impact Alpha" investor dashboard.

### Discouragement of Fraud (The "Stick")
*   **Forensic Slashing**: Confirmed fraud results in immediate loss of staked VUs.
*   **Reputation Decay**: Inactivity or low-quality data submissions lead to a gradual decay in Trust Score.
*   **Shadow-Audit**: Low-trust nodes are subjected to 5x more forensic scrutiny without their knowledge.
*   **Public Red-Flag**: Repeated failures trigger a "Forensic Warning" on the node's public profile.

---

## 2. Reputation & Progress Systems

### Trust Tiers (The "Impact Identity")
1.  **Seed (0.0 - 0.3)**: New entrants. Limited funding, high collateral, 100% audit rate.
2.  **Sprout (0.3 - 0.6)**: Verified history. Standard funding, moderate collateral, 50% audit rate.
3.  **Harvest (0.6 - 0.9)**: Proven reliability. Priority funding, low collateral, 10% audit rate.
4.  **Guardian (0.9 - 1.0)**: Protocol pillars. Instant funding, zero collateral, governance leadership.

### Progress Signals
*   **Trust Milestone Bar**: A visual indicator showing exactly how many high-quality PoIs are needed for the next tier.
*   **"Impact Streak"**: Rewards for consistent, daily high-quality data submissions.
*   **Trust Unlock Notifications**: "Congratulations! You've unlocked 'Instant Tranche Release' by reaching Harvest Tier."

---

## 3. Subtle Nudges & Feedback Loops

### The "Data Density" Nudge
*   **Context**: During PoI submission.
*   **Nudge**: "High-trust nodes in your region usually include 3+ photos. Adding one more could boost your Trust Score by 0.05."
*   **Psychology**: Uses social proof and loss aversion (missing out on a boost).

### The "Forensic Feedback" Loop
*   **Context**: Post-verification.
*   **Feedback**: "Your last PoI was verified with 99.8% confidence. +5 VU Reward issued."
*   **Psychology**: Immediate positive reinforcement for high-quality work.

---

## 4. Why Users WANT to Improve Their Trust Score

**"How to make users WANT to improve their trust score?"**
1.  **Economic Access**: Higher scores directly translate to more capital, faster. It's a financial necessity.
2.  **Social Status**: The "Guardian" badge is a mark of prestige in the local and global community.
3.  **Reduced Friction**: High trust means less "paperwork" (audits) and more "doing" (impact).
4.  **Impact Legacy**: A high Trust Score is a permanent, cryptographic record of a user's contribution to the world.

---

## 5. UI Reinforcement Patterns

*   **The "Trust Halo"**: A subtle glow around the profile pictures of Guardian-tier nodes.
*   **Contextual Encouragement**: "Your data is helping 1,200 investors trust this project. Keep it up!"
*   **The "Next Unlock" Preview**: Always showing the next benefit the user is working toward (e.g., "0.15 more to unlock Governance").
