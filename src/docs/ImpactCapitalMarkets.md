# KulimaVerse: Impact Capital Markets (ICM) Layer

The ICM Layer transforms verified Proof of Impact (PoI) into **Investment-Grade Impact Assets**. It bridges the gap between philanthropic capital and institutional finance by providing a transparent, forensic, and liquid market for social and environmental outcomes.

---

## 1. From PoI to Investment-Grade Assets

### The Verification Pipeline
1.  **Raw PoI**: GPS-tagged photos, sensor data, and labor logs.
2.  **Forensic Audit**: The Forensic Agent validates the data against global fraud patterns.
3.  **Trust Attestation**: The project's Trust Score (TS) is factored in.
4.  **Impact Assetization**: Verified outcomes are bundled into **Impact Tokens (ITs)** or **Outcome Units (OUs)**.

### Risk Scoring (IRS - Impact Risk Score)
Projects are assigned an IRS (0.00 - 1.00) based on:
*   **Historical TS**: Past performance of the implementing NGO/Node.
*   **Data Density**: The volume and variety of PoI submitted.
*   **Environmental Volatility**: Regional climate or political risk factors.

---

## 2. Financial Products

### A. Impact Bonds (IBs)
*   **Structure**: Investors provide upfront capital for a project.
*   **Trigger**: Repayment is triggered by the verification of specific PoI milestones.
*   **Yield**: Interest is paid by "Outcome Funders" (Governments, Philanthropies) upon successful impact delivery.

### B. Outcome-Based Investments (OBIs)
*   **Structure**: Direct equity or debt in a project where returns are pegged to impact performance.
*   **Mechanism**: If a project exceeds its impact targets (verified by PoI), the investor receives a "Performance Bonus" from the protocol treasury.

---

## 3. Investor Flow & Return Mechanisms

### The Flow
1.  **Capital Injection**: Investor stakes USDC/VU into a project pool.
2.  **Deployment**: Capital is released to the NGO based on their Trust Tier.
3.  **Impact Generation**: NGO performs work and submits PoI.
4.  **Verification**: Forensic Agent and Citizen Validators verify the impact.
5.  **Outcome Payment**: Outcome Funders deposit funds into the pool based on verified ITs.
6.  **Distribution**: Principal + Yield is returned to the investor.

### Return Mechanisms
*   **Fixed Yield**: Standard interest for Impact Bonds.
*   **Impact Alpha**: Additional returns generated by "Impact Credits" (e.g., Carbon Credits) sold on secondary markets.

---

## 4. Safeguards & Fraud Protection

*   **Collateralized Trust**: High-trust nodes must stake VU to participate in capital markets.
*   **Forensic Slashing**: If fraud is detected post-funding, the node's stake is slashed and used to compensate investors.
*   **Multi-Sig Release**: Capital is released in tranches only after PoI verification of the previous stage.

---

## 5. Why Investors Trust This System

**"What makes investors trust this system?"**
1.  **Forensic Transparency**: Investors can drill down from a portfolio-level metric to the individual GPS-tagged photo that generated it.
2.  **Deterministic Returns**: Returns are not based on "self-reported" PDFs but on cryptographic proof.
3.  **Risk Mitigation**: The Trust Score system systematically filters out low-integrity actors before they can access capital.

## 6. Example Scenario: The "Zambia Maize Bond"
*   **Goal**: Increase yield for 1,000 smallholders by 20%.
*   **Investment**: $1M upfront from an Impact Fund.
*   **PoI**: Weekly soil sensor data + harvest weight verification.
*   **Outcome Funder**: Ministry of Agriculture (Zambia) + Global Climate Fund.
*   **Result**: 22% yield increase verified. Investors receive 8% IRR + 2% "Impact Alpha" bonus.
